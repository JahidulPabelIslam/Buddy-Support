Help

On Start Page.

  1. Choose whether you are a Supporter Or want Support
  2. Select what you want to talk about.
  3. Click (Insert The Same Looking Button) to Find a Match.

On Chat Page.

  • You can Type into (Insert Same Input) and Send a text Message by clicking Enter or (Insert Same Button).
  • You can Skip the matched user by clicking on (Insert Same Button).
  • You can Report the matched user by clicking on (Insert Same Button).
  • You can invite the other user to a game, you can choose by clicking on (Insert Same Button) then click on your chosen game.

If you skip, report, get skipped, reported or blocked you will be redirected to start page where you can can change your options and rematch.

On Contact Form Page.

You can use the form to send us a enqiry or some feedback.

  • You will have to enter your email address.
  • You can enter a subject for the message.
  • You will have to enter your message.
